Honey Ginger Tea with Milk and Lemon

very tasty milk tea with honey and ginger, good to drink when you are sick

How to make?

  1. chop the ginger
  2. cut the lemmon into small pieces
  3. boil the milk
  4. make tea with hot water
  5. add ginger, lemon and milk to the tea
  6. let it sit for 10 min
